📊 Sales Performance, Planning & Coverage Dashboard (Power BI)
📌 Overview

This Power BI dashboard delivers a full business performance view combining:

Sales vs Plan analysis,
Profitability (Margin & Cost structure),
Channel performance (TT, KA, etc.),
Regional and customer coverage,
Product and category insights,
Sales team performance

It is designed for distribution and FMCG environments, supporting both strategic and operational decision-making.

🎯 Key KPIs

Total Sales – 153M,
Total Sales with Discount – 110M,
Total Discount – 20M,
Total Quantity – 17M,

📊 Dashboard Structure

1. 📈 Sales vs Plan Performance

Total Sales vs Total Plan,
% Achievement,
Sales vs Previous Year,
YoY growth metrics

👉 Helps answer:

Are we meeting targets?
Which companies/regions underperform vs plan?

2. 🏢 Company Performance Analysis

Sales, Cost, Margin by Company
Includes:
Sales vs Previous Year
% Total Cost
Margin evolution
YoY Cost & Sales Change

👉 Business value:

Identify top clients,
Detect profitability issues per company

3. 🌍 Regional Performance

Sales by Region (map + table),
Regional plan vs actual,
YoY growth comparison

👉 Helps:

Evaluate geographic performance,
Allocate resources better

4. 🛒 Sales Channel Analysis

Channels: KA, TT, PH, EC

Metrics:
Total Sales,
Plan vs Actual,
Margin,
YoY Growth

👉 Insight:

Compare Traditional Trade vs Key Accounts,
Identify most profitable channel

5. 🏷️ Brand & Category Analysis
Sales by Brand

Category-level breakdown:
Automotive,
Electronics,
Fashion,
Beverages

👉 Helps:

Understand portfolio performance,
Identify growth categories

6. 📦 Product Performance

Sales & Quantity by product,
YoY Sales & Quantity growth

👉 Helps:

Identify top-selling products,
Detect growth drivers

7. 📅 Monthly Performance
Monthly Plan vs Actual,
% Achievement,
Cost structure trends

👉 Helps:

Track seasonality,
Monitor execution consistency

8. 👥 Sales Team Performance
Sales by Sales Agent,
Manager-level performance,
Plan vs Actual vs YoY growth

👉 Helps:

Evaluate team productivity,
Identify high/low performers

9. 📍 Coverage & Distribution Analysis
   
Total coverage plan vs actual,
Unique buyers per agent/brand,
Distribution gaps (“No distribution” insights)

👉 Helps:

Detect market coverage gaps,
Improve distribution strategy

10. 💸 Cost Structure Analysis
Breakdown of:
Retro,
Scandata,
Cashback,
Marketing

👉 Helps:

Understand where money is spent,
Optimize cost efficiency

11. 🤖 Key Influencers (AI Insights)

Identifies factors driving high sales performance

Example:

Higher discounts → higher probability of sales increase,
Sales channel impacts performance

👉 Helps:

Quickly understand what drives results

🎛️ Filters (Slicers)

Brand,
Company,
Region,
Sales Channel,
Year / Quarter / Month,
Manager

👉 Fully interactive exploration across all dimensions
